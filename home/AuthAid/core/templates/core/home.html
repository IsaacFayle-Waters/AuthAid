.authAid is a platform designed to encourage and assist authors in planning out the stories they are - or will be - working on.

It can be difficult to keep all the elements of a narrative tethered together in one's mind. .authAid seeks to assist in this effort, by providing an intuitive, flexible, framework for the creative mind.

Whether you want to jot down the outline of a character, or construct an entire universe with multiple connected narratives, .authAid is positioned to assist.
